5 Minute Dip Recipe:

  • 5 green onions chopped
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups mayo
  • 1 1/2 bacon bits
  • 1/2 cup silver almonds
  • - - - Mix ingredients and chill for 2 hours before serving - - -
