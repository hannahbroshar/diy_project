Dinner Party: Set-Up Page

What You'll Need:

  • Invitations with time, date & location of dinner!
  • Address Book
  • Tablecloth
  • Silverware
  • Plates and Cups
  • A bouquet of flowers for table decoration

Instructions:

Depending on what you are hosting for, invitations should be sent a month in advance. Your invitation should include time, date location of the party and RSVP option. Once you have your head count, you can pick up the rest of your items at your local department store.
